InputValidator

Sample Attribute: data-rule="^[a-zA-Z,.]{2,20}$"

Invalid examples: Smith123 or Paul_Harris
Invalid examples: aaa-aaa-aaaa or 123a
Invalid examples: smith@bad_email.com or @example.com
